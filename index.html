OUR STORY

Bringing the Heart of Ibadan
To Every Table

At Amala Ibadan, every meal carries the warmth of tradition and the richness of authentic Yoruba cuisine. We honour generations of recipes while delivering an experience that feels modern, welcoming, and unforgettable.

From carefully sourced ingredients to expertly prepared soups, swallows, and proteins, every plate reflects our commitment to quality, consistency, and genuine hospitality.

"Good food is more than a meal—it's a memory shared with the people you love."

LEADERSHIP

The Vision Behind
Amala Ibadan

Mr. and Mrs. Salako founded Amala Ibadan with a simple vision—to preserve the rich traditions of authentic Yoruba cuisine while delivering an exceptional dining experience built on quality, consistency, and genuine hospitality.

Through passion, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, they have grown Amala Ibadan into a trusted destination for delicious Nigerian meals enjoyed by individuals, families, and businesses alike.

"Every meal should leave a lasting memory long after the last bite."
