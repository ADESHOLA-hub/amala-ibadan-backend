Who We Are

Crafted with culture,
served with love

At Amala Ibadan, our signature dish is more than food — it's heritage. Our Amala, made from yam flour, is prepared to perfection and paired with rich soups like Gbegiri, Ewedu, and slow-cooked stews. Each plate is a celebration of Yoruba tradition.

From smoky Suya to flavourful Jollof Rice, every dish on our menu honours Nigeria's diverse culinary regions — made fresh daily, served with genuine warmth.

Leadership

Meet the Founders

Mr. and Mrs. Salako are the visionary leaders behind Amala Ibadan. With over four years of experience in Nigeria's culinary industry, they have grown the brand into a household name — known for authentic flavours, consistency, and a genuine dedication to customer satisfaction.

Their mission is simple: to make every customer feel at home while enjoying premium-quality Nigerian meals prepared with passion.

Every plate we serve is a reflection of who we are — our culture, our family, our pride.
