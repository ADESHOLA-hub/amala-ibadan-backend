🙋‍♀️ Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Explore common inquiries below.

🚚 Do you deliver? +
Yes! We deliver across Lagos and beyond. Order via ChowDeck or chat with us on WhatsApp.
📦 How do I place an order? +
You can order via ChowDeck or send us a direct message on WhatsApp. Just pick your meals, and we’ll do the rest.
🌶 Are your soups spicy? +
Most of our soups are medium-spiced. Let us know during ordering if you prefer mild or extra spicy!
✨ Can I customize my package? +
Absolutely! We offer custom combos, especially for bulk or festive orders. Just reach out on WhatsApp.
⚠️ Do your meals contain allergens? +
Some dishes may include fish, crayfish, or peanuts. Kindly inform us if you have any allergies.
